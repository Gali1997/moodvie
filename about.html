A note from me

Hi, I'm Galina.

Moodvie is something I'm building while I'm learning how to make stuff. I'm in the middle of a course called Hustle Badger, and this is the first thing I've made that I actually wanted to use myself.

The honest version: you could open ChatGPT or Claude right now, type the same thing into it, and get a movie recommendation. I know. I do that too sometimes. So I want to be straight with you about what makes this different — or at least, what I'm trying to make different.

I wanted a small, focused thing. A single page that does one thing well. Not a chat where you have to phrase the prompt right. Not a list of forty options sorted by what's popular this week. Just a quiet little corner of the internet that asks how you feel and hands you back one film. With a real reason why. Like a friend would.

And I wanted it to feel like something. Most streaming apps look like a spreadsheet. Movie nights deserve better than a spreadsheet.

If you find a film here that hits, I'd genuinely love to know. And if it gets it weirdly wrong, tell me that too — every strange mood you throw at it teaches me how to make it better.

Thanks for being one of the first people here. It means a lot.

Moodvie is free, and it'll stay free. If you ever feel like saying thanks, there's a buy me a coffee link — but please don't feel like you have to. Honestly.

— with love, Galina Skopje · 2026
