How it works

01 · Mood 02 · Match 03 · Watch
01

Step one · Mood

Type how you feel. However it comes out of you.

A single word. A messy paragraph. An oddly specific Sunday night. There's no wrong way to describe what you're feeling — and no judgment about how raw or weird it sounds.

02

Step two · Match

Our AI reads between the lines of what you wrote.

Not keywords. Not your watch history. Not what's trending. Moodvie picks up on the actual tone of what you said — the texture, the longing, the specific shape of where you are tonight — and matches it to one film.

03

Step three · Watch

One film. A real reason why. Where to watch it.

A single recommendation — with the poster, the trailer, and every streaming service it's playing on tonight. Plus a few sentences in plain language about why this one, for you, right now. Save it for later or ask for another. That's it.
